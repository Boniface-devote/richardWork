About Us

Welcome to HIV Care Uganda

Empowering Hope Uganda was established in September 2024 and based in Kakindu, Mityana District, Uganda. Empowering Hope Uganda is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the well-being of marginalized communities. Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive environment where all individuals, especially those facing social and economic challenges, can thrive.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including healthcare, educational support, home care, and childcare initiatives. By addressing critical needs and promoting holistic development, we empower families to build sustainable and resilient futures. Our efforts extend to fostering access to quality education for children, providing compassionate home-based care, and enhancing food security through agricultural development projects.

Empowering Hope Uganda is driven by a commitment to collaboration, innovation, and compassion. Our diverse team of professionals and volunteers brings together unique skills and a shared dedication to making a lasting impact. Through strategic partnerships and community-based interventions, we continuously strive to uplift underserved populations and transform lives.

oin us on this transformative journey and become part of a movement that is redefining hope and changing futures. The impact we create together today will echo in the generations to come—because at Empowering Hope Uganda, every action ignites a legacy of change.

Our Management Team

Meet the dedicated team members who are committed to advancing our mission and supporting those affected by HIV in Uganda.

Kawuma Umaruh

Founder

Umar is a dedicated and accomplished medical professional with extensive experience in providing quality healthcare services, particularly in the management of HIV/AIDS and childhood illnesses. Trained as a medical practitioner, Umar possesses expertise in:

  • Integrated Management of Childhood Illnesses (IMCI).
  • Emergency response, including surgical and medical first aid.
  • Safe Male Circumcision (SMC) as an HIV prevention strategy.
  • Diagnosis and management of HIV/AIDS.
    • With a strong passion for community health, Kawuma has worked tirelessly to improve health outcomes, especially among vulnerable populations. His commitment to delivering compassionate and evidence-based care was the drive for this initiative to Empower as many individuals as possible to live improved and purposeful lives.

    Kyambadde Richard

    Deputy Director

    Richard, the Deputy Director at Empowering Hope Uganda, plays a pivotal role in the organization, bringing his expertise in Agricultural Engineering to support sustainable development projects. His background enables him to design and implement agricultural initiatives that improve food security and promote economic self-reliance among marginalized communities. Richard's strategic approach, combined with his commitment to reducing stigma and enhancing access to essential resources, makes him an invaluable asset to the organization's mission.

    Namwanje Prossie

    Project manager

    Prossie is the Project Manager at Empowering Hope Uganda, where she leads various initiatives aimed at empowering and uplifting marginalized communities. With a background in Land Surveying, she brings a unique perspective and valuable technical skills to the organization's development projects. Her dedication to creating sustainable and impactful solutions ensures that the programs are both effective and aligned with the needs of the communities they serve. Prossie’s leadership and expertise are vital in driving Empowering Hope Uganda’s mission forward.
