About Us

Welcome to HIV Care Uganda

We are dedicated to providing education, support, and healthcare services to people living with HIV in Uganda. Our mission is to raise awareness about HIV prevention and treatment, fight the stigma, and ensure everyone has access to the support they need to live healthy, fulfilling lives.

Through partnerships with healthcare professionals, community outreach programs, and support groups, we empower individuals and families affected by HIV to take control of their health. Together, we aim to build a future where HIV no longer holds people back from living their best lives.

Join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of those affected by HIV in Uganda. Whether it's through donations, volunteering, or spreading awareness, every effort counts.
